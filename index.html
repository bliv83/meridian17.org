Advancing African-European Partnership

Why we exist

There is a missing middle in how we think about Africa-Europe cooperation.

Most think tanks approach this relationship through the lens of policy or development. Neither puts business at the centre - yet business is where policy either works or doesn't. GDP, employment, investment: these are all measures of how well business operates. We call this gap the Missing Middle, and it is what Meridian17 is here to address.
